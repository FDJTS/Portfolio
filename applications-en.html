Al-Muslim App 📖

A comprehensive Islamic application for smartphones

Description:

Al-Muslim is an advanced Islamic application designed specifically for Muslims seeking a comprehensive app that combines religious and practical tools. The app provides a seamless and easy-to-use experience with a professional interface and modern design.

Key Features:

  • ⏰ Accurate prayer times based on your geographic location
  • 📅 Comprehensive prayer schedule
  • 🕋 Complete Quran browser
  • 📿 Daily and traditional Adhkar (remembrances)
  • 📆 Hijri calendar with date conversion
  • 🧭 Qibla compass with smart compass technology
  • 🔔 Smart customizable notifications
  • 📍 Accurate location detection with advanced GPS technology
  • 🌓 Dark and light mode support
  • ⚙️ Advanced customizable settings

Supported Platforms:

Currently available on Android. Work is underway on iOS and Web versions.

Technology Stack:

React Native Expo TypeScript EAS Build Geolocation API SQLite

App Structure:

  • 🏠 Home Screen - Display current prayer times
  • 📅 Prayer Schedule - Detailed view of all prayer times
  • 📖 Quran - Interactive Quranic text browser
  • 📿 Adhkar - Comprehensive collection of Islamic remembrances
  • 📆 Hijri Calendar - Islamic date tracking
  • 🧭 Qibla Compass - Determine Qibla direction accurately
  • ⚙️ Settings - Full app customization
