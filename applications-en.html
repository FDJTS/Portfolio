Al-Muslim (The All-in-One Companion)

A clean, interactive Islamic experience for your phone

The Real Talk:

Al-Muslim isn't just another app—it's built for Muslims who want a clean, no-nonsense tool that actually feels modern. No clutter, just the features you need, wrapped in an interface that actually slaps.

Everything Inside:

  • On-point prayer times based on where you are
  • Your full prayer schedule, sorted
  • Quran browser that actually feels smooth
  • All the essential Adhkar for your day
  • Hijri calendar (converter included)
  • Qibla compass that stays accurate
  • Notifications that don't annoy you
  • Zero-stress location detection
  • Aesthetic Dark/Light modes
  • Settings you can actually customize

Supported Platforms:

Currently available on Android. Work is underway on iOS and Web versions.

Technology Stack:

React Native Expo TypeScript EAS Build Geolocation API SQLite

App Structure:

  • Home Screen - Display current prayer times
  • Prayer Schedule - Detailed view of all prayer times
  • Quran - Interactive Quranic text browser
  • Adhkar - Comprehensive collection of Islamic remembrances
  • Hijri Calendar - Islamic date tracking
  • Qibla Compass - Determine Qibla direction accurately
  • Settings - Full app customization
